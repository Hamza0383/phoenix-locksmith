Car Key Types We Replace

Standard Metal Keys (Older Vehicles)

Most cars before 1995:

Just a metal key, no chip or electronics. We cut it from your VIN or existing key. Fastest and cheapest option.

Cost: $75-$150

Time: 15-20 minutes

Transponder Chip Keys (1995-2015)

Most common type we replace.

Looks like a regular key but has a chip in the plastic head. We cut the metal part AND program the chip so your car's immobilizer accepts it.

Works on: Toyota, Honda, Ford, Chevy, Nissan, Dodge, and most brands from mid-90s to mid-2010s

Cost: $150-$250

Time: 25-35 minutes

Key Fobs with Blade (Combo Keys)

Remote buttons with a flip-out or removable key blade.

Common on Honda, Toyota, Nissan, Mazda, Subaru, and others. We replace the entire fob unit and program both the remote functions and the blade.

Cost: $175-$300

Time: 30-40 minutes

Smart Keys / Push-to-Start (2010+)

Proximity keys that let you unlock and start without taking the key out.

Most expensive to replace because of the technology inside. We program these to work with your car's push-button start system.

Common on: Toyota, Lexus, Ford, Chevy, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, and luxury brands

Cost: $200-$400

Time: 35-45 minutes

Luxury / High-Security Keys

BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Porsche, Land Rover:

Some luxury brands require dealer-specific equipment. We handle most models, but certain newer Mercedes and BMW keys need the dealer.

We'll tell you on the phone whether we can program your specific key or if you need to go to the dealer.

How Car Key Replacement Works

Never used a mobile locksmith for car keys? Here's the process:

1. You Call or Text

Tell us what happened (lost keys, need spare, fob broke). Give us your vehicle make, model, year, and location.

We'll confirm we can make your key type and give you a price estimate and ETA.

2. Verify Ownership

When our tech arrives, they'll check:

  • Your photo ID
  • Vehicle registration or title
  • VIN matches the documents

This protects you from someone making keys to your car without permission.

3. Cut the Key

Using your VIN or existing key, we cut a new key on our mobile key-cutting machine. Takes 5-10 minutes.

4. Program the Key

For chip keys, fobs, and smart keys, we connect programming equipment to your car's diagnostic port and sync the new key. Takes 10-20 minutes.

5. Test Everything

We test the new key:

  • Opens the doors
  • Starts the engine
  • Remote buttons work (if applicable)
  • Everything functions correctly

6. You're Done

Pay with cash or card. We give you a receipt. Drive away with your new working key.

Total time: 30-45 minutes for most vehicles

What You Need to Get a Replacement Key

To Prove You Own the Vehicle:

You MUST have one of these:

  • Vehicle registration (most common)
  • Title (if you have it)
  • Lease agreement (if leasing)

Plus:

  • Photo ID (license, state ID, passport)
  • VIN (usually visible on dashboard or driver's door jamb)

Can't find your registration?

  • Check glove box
  • Check insurance card (often has VIN)
  • VIN is printed on dashboard (visible through windshield)
  • DMV can provide duplicate registration

We can't make keys without proof of ownership. This protects you from theft.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a car key?

Typical timeline:

  • Arrival: 20-35 minutes (depends on your location)
  • Cutting the key: 5-10 minutes
  • Programming: 10-20 minutes
  • Testing: 5 minutes

Total: 30-45 minutes from when we arrive to when you have a working key

Do you charge more at night or on weekends?

No. Same pricing 24/7. We don't add surcharges for after-hours, weekends, or holidays for key replacement service.

What if my key fob just needs a new battery?

If it's just a dead battery, we can replace that too (usually $10-20). But if the fob is physically broken or non-responsive even with new battery, you need a new fob ($150-300).

We'll test it when we arrive and tell you which service you need.

Can you program a key fob I bought online?

Sometimes. If you bought a blank fob online, we can often program it for $75-150 (cheaper than buying through us).

Important: The fob must be compatible with your exact vehicle. Many online fobs don't work or need special programming we can't do. Call first before buying online.

How do I know you won't make extra keys to my car?

Security measures:

  • Every key we make is logged with VIN and customer ID
  • We verify ownership every time (registration + photo ID)
  • Only make keys when vehicle owner is present
  • Background-checked technicians

We take vehicle security seriously. You're trusting us with access to your car.
