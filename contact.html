Frequently Asked Questions

What's the fastest way to reach you in an emergency?

Call (602) 744-7531 directly. A live dispatcher answers 24/7 (not voicemail). For non-urgent requests, use the contact form above and we'll respond within a few hours.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Phoenix metro area including Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Glendale, Peoria, Paradise Valley, and surrounding communities. Call with your address and we'll confirm availability.

How fast can you get to me?

Central Phoenix: 15-25 minutes. Scottsdale/Tempe: 20-30 minutes. Mesa/Chandler/Gilbert: 30-40 minutes. Exact times depend on traffic and tech availability. Dispatch gives you a real-time ETA when you call.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. We provide price ranges over the phone for standard services. For larger projects like master key systems or access control, we offer free on-site consultations and written estimates.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover), debit cards, and mobile payment apps. Business accounts with Net 30 terms available for established commercial clients.

Can I schedule service in advance?

Yes. Call or use the contact form to schedule non-emergency services like rekeying, lock installation, or master key systems. We offer same-day, next-day, and after-hours appointments to fit your schedule.
