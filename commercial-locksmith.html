Master Key Systems

Tired of carrying 15 keys? Need different access levels for managers and staff?

Master key systems give you control over who accesses which doors in your building.

How it works:

You get a master key that opens every door in your system. Each door also has its own individual key that ONLY opens that specific door.

Example setup:

  • Owner: Master key (opens all doors)
  • Manager: Sub-master key (opens front door, office, stockroom)
  • Employee: Individual key (opens front door only)
  • Cleaning crew: Individual key (opens front door, bathrooms)

Best for:

  • Multi-room offices
  • Retail stores with back rooms
  • Buildings with restricted areas
  • Property management (multiple tenants)
  • Businesses with employee turnover

Process:

  1. We survey your building (which doors, who needs access)
  2. Design key hierarchy on paper
  3. Get your approval
  4. Rekey or install locks to match system
  5. Cut and label all keys
  6. Provide key tracking sheets

Timeline:

  • Consultation: 1 hour
  • Small office (5-10 doors): 1 day
  • Larger building (10-20 doors): 2-3 days

Cost:

  • Design and consultation: $150-250
  • Installation per door: $85-120 first lock, $40-60 each additional
  • Typical 8-door office: $800-1,200 complete

High-security option: We can use restricted keyway systems where keys can't be copied at hardware stores (requires authorization from us).

Commercial Rekeying

Employee quit or got fired? Lost keys? Just bought a commercial property?

Rekeying changes your locks so old keys stop working. Much cheaper than replacing all the hardware.

When businesses rekey:

  • Employee leaves (especially if they left on bad terms)
  • Lost or stolen keys
  • Purchased existing business location
  • Contractor had keys and project is done
  • Want to consolidate multiple key types to one

Process:

  • We come to your business (can work after hours if needed)
  • Remove lock cylinders from doors
  • Change pin configuration (renders old keys useless)
  • Cut new keys on-site
  • Test everything
  • Give you 3-5 copies of new keys

Time: 10-15 minutes per door

Cost:

  • First lock: $95-$140
  • Additional locks: $35-50 each
  • 5-door office: $235-340
  • 10-door retail space: $410-590

Volume discount: 10+ doors get discounted per-lock pricing.

After-hours service available: We can work nights/weekends so we don't disrupt your business. No surcharge for after-hours scheduling (only emergency calls different).

Lock Repair & Maintenance

Door not latching right? Lock sticking? Key hard to turn?

Phoenix heat and heavy use are tough on commercial locks. We repair most issues on-site.

Common problems we fix:

  • Keys that won't turn smoothly

  • Implementation: 1-3 days depending on number of doors
  • Total: Usually 1 week from consultation to completion

What's the difference between rekeying and replacing locks?

Rekeying: Changes the internal pins so old keys don't work. Same lock hardware stays in place. Costs less. Takes less time.

Replacing: Removes old locks entirely and installs new ones. Costs more. Takes longer. Necessary if locks are damaged, worn out, or you want to upgrade to better security.

We'll check your locks and recommend which makes sense for your situation.
